You begin this HIT with an initial endowment of 10 ECU.

To determine your final earnings, we will randomly choose one of the seven stages. Your total earnings at the end of this HIT are the earnings from that stage plus your initial endowment of 10 ECU. Your earnings from each stage are determined by the values of your draws.

After filling out the questionnaire, all earned ECU from the randomly chosen stage plus your initial endowment will be converted into Dollars.
