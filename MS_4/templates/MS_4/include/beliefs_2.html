You can earn additional bonus money for both sets of questions, depending on how accurate your estimates are.

Payment First Set of Questions:

You will earn an additional 50 ECU if you rank all three options for all four metrics correctly. If you correctly rank all three options for three metrics, you will earn an additional 25 ECU. Otherwise, you will not earn any additional bonus money.

Payment Second Set of Questions:

For each of the four metrics, you will be paid additional bonus money depending on how close you estimations are to the options true values.

  • Estimation within 5% of the true value: 15 ECU
  • Estimation within 10% of the true value: 10 ECU
  • Estimation within 15% of the true value: 5 ECU
  • Otherwise: 0 ECU

Thus, in total, you can earn additional bonus money of up to 60 ECU (15 ECU for each metric).
