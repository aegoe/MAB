Remember:

"Mean" refers to an option's average payoff per draw.

"SD" refers to an option's standard deviation. The standard deviation is a measure of how spread out the draws are in relation to the option's average. A low standard deviation means that most numbers are close to the average. A high standard deviation means that numbers are more widely dispersed.

"Lowest Draw" refers to the Coin with the lowest value for a particular option.

"Highest Draw" refers to the Coin with the highest value for a particular option.
