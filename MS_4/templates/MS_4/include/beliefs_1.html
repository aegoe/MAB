You will now complete two sets of questions. Depending on the accuracy of your answers, you can earn additional bonus money.

First Set of Questions: You will be asked to arrange the three options you just sampled through in a descending order according to four different metrics:

(1) "Mean": refers to an option's average payoff per draw.

(2) "SD": refers to an option's standard deviation. The standard deviation is a measure of how spread out the draws are in relation to the option's average. A low standard deviation means that most numbers are close to the average. A high standard deviation means that numbers are more widely dispersed.

(3) "Lowest Draw": refers to the Coin with the lowest value for a particular option.

(4) "Highest Draw": refers to the Coin with the highest value for a particular option.

Thus, you will be asked to rank the three options with regard to their average payoff, their standard deviation, their lowest possible draw and their highest possible draw.

Second Set of Questions: You will be asked to estimate the specific mean for each option.
