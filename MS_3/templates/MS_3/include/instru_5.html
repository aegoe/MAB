Stage "TWO"

In order to learn more about the options, stage "TWO" consists of 100 sampling rounds, where you can draw more coins from each option by investing points. These coins will not affect your final payoff, but they will give you additional information about the potential value of each option. <\p>

At the beginning of each round, you receive one point. You can invest this point freely between all three options, by directly clicking on the button below the option. Points cannot be accumulated and therefore, each point must be invested in each round. After submitting your sampling investment, a feedback screen informs you about the value of the coin drawn (see image below).
