The Stages


{% endblock %} {% block content %}

In this HIT, you will complete two stages. In each stage, you will choose among same set of different options to receive Coins.

At the beginning of stage "ONE", you will learn about the options by observing provided information. Afterwards, you make your choice that determines your bonus payoff. Sate "ONE" is than finsihed and your directly forwarded to stage "TWO". Then, you will have the opportunity to learn more by sampling through the options yourself.

At stage "TWO", you will be able to learn more about the options, you already know from stage "ONE", by sampling through them. After you have done sampling, again, you will make another choice that determines your bonus payoff.
